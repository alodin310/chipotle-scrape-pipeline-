# Management — Chipotle Mexican Grill

## Scott Boatwright — Chief Executive Officer

**Scott Boatwright** is the Chief Executive Officer and a member of the Board of Directors for Chipotle Mexican Grill. Appointed in November 2024, Mr. Boatwright previously served as Interim Chief Executive Officer since August 2024. Prior to that, he held the role of Chief Operating Officer/Chief Restaurant Officer since May 2017. Mr. Boatwright was named "Operations CREATOR of the Year" by Nation's Restaurant News in 2021 and named to Fast Casual's "2020 Top 25 Executives" list.

Prior to Chipotle, Mr. Boatwright spent 18 years with Arby's Restaurant Group in various leadership positions. He holds a Master of Business Administration from the J. Mack Robinson College of Business at Georgia State University.

## Adam Rymer — Chief Financial Officer

**Adam Rymer** is the Chief Financial Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill, responsible for corporate and field financial planning and analysis, as well as the financial reporting, tax, treasury and investor relations functions. He previously served as Vice President of Finance and has held a variety of positions in Finance and People Experience since joining the organization in 2009. In 2025, Mr. Rymer was named Extel All America Best CFO for the restaurant sector.

Mr. Rymer has a Bachelor of Business Administration degree in finance from the University of Texas at Austin.

## Curt Garner — President, Chief Strategy and Technology Officer

**Curt Garner** is the President, Chief Strategy and Technology Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill. Prior to this, he served as the Chief Customer and Technology Officer for nearly a decade since joining the organization in November 2015. In 2020, Mr. Garner was named to the CIO Hall of Fame, and in 2025, he was featured on Forbes's CIO Next list.

Prior to Chipotle, Mr. Garner worked at Starbucks Corporation for 17 years, most recently serving as Executive Vice President and Chief Technology Officer. Mr. Garner has a Bachelor of Arts degree in economics from The Ohio State University.

## Laurie Schalow — Chief Corporate Affairs and Food Safety Officer

**Laurie Schalow** is the Chief Corporate Affairs and Food Safety Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill. In her role, Ms. Schalow oversees all internal and external communications, stakeholder engagement, sustainability, customer service, social listening, government affairs, food safety and quality assurance for the brand's 4,000+ restaurants.

Prior to joining Chipotle in 2017, Ms. Schalow was Vice President of Public Affairs for Yum! Brands. Ms. Schalow holds a Bachelor of Science degree in business from Miami University and received her Master of Business Administration from Case Western Reserve and Wayne State University.

## Ilene Eskenazi — Chief Legal and Human Resources Officer

**Ilene Eskenazi** is the Chief Legal and Human Resources Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill. Appointed in January 2026, Ms. Eskenazi previously served as the Chief Human Resources Officer for two years at the organization.

Prior to joining Chipotle, Ms. Eskenazi served as the Chief Legal and Human Resources Officer for Petco Health and Wellness Company. She holds a Juris Doctorate from the University of California, Los Angeles' School of Law as well as a Bachelor of Arts degree in philosophy from the University of Michigan.

## Jason Kidd — Chief Operating Officer

**Jason Kidd** is the Chief Operating Officer at Chipotle. He joined the organization in 2025 and is responsible for overseeing operations of the company's 4,000+ restaurants as well as development for its new restaurant openings.

Prior to Chipotle, Mr. Kidd served as the Global Chief Operating Officer at Taco Bell. Mr. Kidd holds a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from the Sam M. Walton College of Business at the University of Arkansas.

## Kerry Bridges — Chief Food Safety Officer

**Kerry Bridges** is the Chief Food Safety Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill. Appointed in January 2026, Ms. Bridges previously served as Vice President of Food Safety. Prior to joining Chipotle, Ms. Bridges oversaw supplier food safety for Wal-Mart. She attended California Polytechnic State University, where she received a bachelor's degree in food science.

## Stephanie Perdue — Senior Vice President of Brand Marketing

**Stephanie Perdue** is the Senior Vice President of Brand Marketing, overseeing brand strategy, advertising, innovation, experiential, social media and brand activation. Prior to Chipotle, Ms. Perdue served as the Chief Marketing Officer at TGI Fridays. She holds an MBA from the UCLA Anderson School of Management.

## Nate Lawton — Chief Business Development Officer

**Nate Lawton** is the Chief Business Development Officer for Chipotle Mexican Grill, overseeing international business development opportunities, emerging concepts and partnership strategy. Prior to joining Chipotle, Mr. Lawton spent over 20 years at Procter & Gamble. He holds a bachelor's degree in business administration from Emory University's Goizueta Business School.

## Nevielle Panthaky — Senior Vice President of Culinary

**Nevielle Panthaky** is the Senior Vice President of Culinary at Chipotle Mexican Grill, overseeing culinary strategy, menu innovation, product commercialization and cross-functional collaboration. A classically trained chef who joined the organization in 2018, Mr. Panthaky holds an MBA from the University of California, Irvine's Paul Merage School of Business.
